General Information

Fees: There is no cost to you for Field Agent assistance.

Tax Matters: Settlement administrators do not provide tax advice. For questions regarding potential tax treatment, consult a qualified tax professional.

Government Benefits: Settlement processing is separate from benefit determinations. For benefit-related concerns, contact the appropriate agency or advisor.

Payment Timing: Settlement distributions occur only after completion of required documentation and applicable approval procedures.

Deceased Claimants / Estate Matters

If the individual named on the documents is deceased, settlement rights may pass to the Estate or legally authorized representative.

In such cases, supporting documentation may be requested, including:

  • Death Certificate
  • Court appointment documents (if applicable)
  • Valid identification of the signing party
